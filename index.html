Yuliya Kind -real estate project

MOTIVATION

The Data Wizards have read many news articles that stated San Francisco Bay Area Real Estate prices are high and growing exponentially . We wanted to find out for ourselves if that is true so we could decide if we wanted to move elsewhere in our retirement. We identified 4 counties that we thought would represent California well (Shasta, Fresno, SF, LA) and looked at the recent 5 years for trends (2014-2018).
